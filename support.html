Frequently asked questions

How do I restore purchases?

Open the Pro Pack section in the game and tap Restore Purchases.

Do I need an account to play?

No. Tinker Shop does not require a separate game account.

Where is my progress stored?

Your progress is stored locally on your device unless you choose to use Apple platform features that sync data.

How do I report a bug?

Email hello@springdata.uk with steps to reproduce, screenshots if available, and your device details.

For Apple App Review

Tinker Shop does not require sign-in for core gameplay. App Review may access the full playable experience without creating an account.

Review questions can be sent to hello@springdata.uk.
